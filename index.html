Welcome to Weiti Midwives

Experienced Hibiscus Coast midwives here to make sure you get the best care during & after your pregnancy.

Congratulations on your pregnancy and welcome!

We are a team of experienced midwives who work together, to provide midwifery care 24 hours/7 days a week.
Midwives in the community provide Primary Care which includes antenatal care, labour and birth, and postnatal visits at your home for mum and baby.
We provide care for our clients at North Shore Hospital, Warkworth Birthing Unit, and support safe homebirths. We work in partnership with you and the Secondary Care Obstetric team at North Shore Hospital when needed by you.
Our clinics are located at Orewa House and Silverdale Medical Centre.
